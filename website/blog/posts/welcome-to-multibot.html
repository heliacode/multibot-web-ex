Welcome to MultiBot

January 16, 2025 3 min read

Welcome to MultiBot! We're excited to have you here. MultiBot is a powerful Twitch bot management platform designed to help streamers create amazing experiences for their audiences.

What is MultiBot?

MultiBot is a comprehensive platform that gives you complete control over your Twitch bot. Whether you want to create custom audio commands, add GIF reactions, integrate with OBS, or manage your chat interactions, MultiBot has you covered.

Getting Started

Getting started with MultiBot is easy:

  1. Sign in with your Twitch account
  2. Navigate to your dashboard
  3. Start creating your first command
  4. Integrate with OBS if needed

Key Features

MultiBot offers a wide range of features to enhance your stream:

  • Audio Commands: Create custom sound effects that trigger from chat
  • GIF Commands: Add animated GIFs to your stream with custom triggers
  • OBS Integration: Seamlessly integrate with OBS Studio using browser sources
  • Chat Management: Monitor and interact with your chat in real-time
  • Design Canvas: Visual tools to create and position stream elements

What's Next?

We're constantly working on new features and improvements. Stay tuned to our blog for updates, tutorials, and tips to get the most out of MultiBot.

Ready to get started? Head over to your dashboard and start creating amazing experiences for your viewers!
