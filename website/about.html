About MultiBot

Empowering streamers to create amazing Twitch experiences

Our Mission

At MultiBot, we believe that every streamer deserves powerful, easy-to-use tools to engage with their audience and create memorable streaming experiences. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive platform that makes Twitch bot management simple, intuitive, and fun.

We're committed to continuously improving our platform based on feedback from the streaming community, ensuring that MultiBot remains the go-to solution for streamers of all sizes.

What We Do

MultiBot is a comprehensive Twitch bot management platform that gives streamers complete control over their streaming experience. We provide:

  • Custom audio command creation and management
  • GIF command integration with Giphy
  • Real-time chat monitoring and interaction
  • OBS Studio browser source integration
  • Visual design canvas for stream elements
  • Secure token management and encryption
